Gorden Energy & Drainage is a sump pump company located in Olathe KS. The controller of the company needed to upgrade their complex ticketing system originally developed in ACCESS 2003.  CJK Consulting was contacted to assess the design of the program and submit an SLA to completion. Marlainna was hired as a consultant to complete this task and follow the project to completion.  The re-engineering of this complex ticketing systems included converting the back-end from Access to SQL Server tables.  Each phase of development including research, analysis, coding, and testing was done by Marlainna under the direction of the controller of Gordon Energy.  The client was amazed with quality of work and achievements.  The status of this project is on-going
