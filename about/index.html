Who We Are?

Our Mission

WE Are Experienced Professionals

Small Business Solutions (SBS) is operated by experienced professionals in the areas of IT and Sales Management.  We are bringing years of experience under the SBS umbrella.  Our Chief Information Officer (CIO) is Marlainna Francis, an alumni of University of Missouri Kansas City. Over the years, she has acquired strong analytic skills and the ability to extract, collect, organize, analyze, and interpret data in complex data systems.  We are a non-profit company with Christian values that can be trusted with highly sensitive data and time sensitive information   

The CEO of SBS is Willie Francis.  Willie is an alumni of the Nazarene University.  He graduated with a Masters Degree in theology.   He is also a graduate St. Mary’s University and holds an epic-able reputation as a Finance Director over several high profile car-dealerships.  He has worn many hats in that role including Sales Manager and Sale person for many years. SBS is in the best hands when it comes to cost effective solutions that meet the needs of the client and operations overhead.  We want to pass that value to our customers

We started this business to help local churches and 501(3c) companies automate administrative processes at a affordable rate.  We have expanded our services to sales training because we have the skill set in-house to do it. We want to help our community because we realize database administration and training can be costly.  We are here to help you reach your bottom line and save paper doing it!
