2021 Development Project

SBSKCMO is so excited to announce we are going to bring the first gated community to the inner city.  The groundbreaking is scheduled for the first quarter in 2021 due to the pandemic. 

This housing development project is located in the heart of Kansas City, Missouri covering 50 acres of land.  These abandoned buildings and dilapidated land is located on prime real-estate, off 70 hwy.  Visible for everyone to see.  It has created an eye sour  and an attraction for crime for far too long.  This development project is far overdue!   

SBSKCMO has plans to develop this land and restore the vitality and revenue that has been gone for over a decade.  We will build sixty 3-4 bedroom homes with a price ranging from 150’s to 200’s.  This will be a secured gated community with all the amenities of a upscale neighborhood.  We envision other businesses  like grocery stores, restaurants, and gas stations will surely come. This is a pilot program that cannot fail.

SBSKCMO will guarantee home loan ownership for first-time home buyers. SBSKCMO will carry any loans that do not qualify for approval until the approval credit status is met to finance without our help.  Any money applied for lease or rent of a property will be applied at the time of purchase.  We will carry the note and take on all the risk if your credit is not up to industry standard for a home loan.  This is a win-win project for the community and all parties involved.
